Help The Needy!

This project aims at giving food to the needy and impoverished. Help us reach our goal by donating food (cash and kind accepted) and feeding a family or two. The money or food you give us will be sent to people starving all over the world. By donating, you send food to people without access to food in places with food insecurity. The aim of the project is to feed 100 families across the globe. Let us make the world a better place!
